MediBook - Doctor Appointment Booking App

Project Overview

Project Name: MediBook

Duration: 4 weeks

Role: UX/UI Designer

Tools: Figma, Miro, Google Forms, Zoom

Platform: Mobile App (iOS/Android)

Check the prototype MediBook

Problem Statement

Patients struggle with booking doctor appointments due to long wait times on phone calls, limited availability visibility, and lack of convenient rescheduling options. Healthcare providers also face challenges with appointment management and no-shows.

Solution

MediBook is a mobile application that streamlines the appointment booking process, allowing patients to easily find doctors, check availability, book appointments, and manage their healthcare schedule in one place.

Research & Discovery

Market Research

Competitor Analysis: Analyzed 5 major competitors including Zocdoc, Practo, and HealthTap Market Gap: Found that 73% of patients prefer digital booking but only 40% of practices offer it Key Insights: Users want instant confirmation, easy rescheduling, and doctor reviews

User Research

Method: Online surveys (120 responses) + User interviews (8 participants)

Key Findings: 85% of users have experienced frustration with traditional appointment booking 92% would prefer mobile app over phone calls Top pain points: Long hold times, limited availability info, difficulty rescheduling Users want: Doctor profiles, reviews, insurance verification, appointment reminders
