User Research

Method:Interviewed 5 food delivery users (ages 18‚Äì35)

Key Findings:

  • Users want fast ordering and clear menu visualization.
  • They often get frustrated by complex sign-up flows or too many steps before payment.
  • They prefer personalized recommendations and live order tracking.
  • Discounts and combo offers are major motivators.
  • Users want a light, fun design that reflects food happiness and trust.
80% want faster reordering for favorite meals Top pain points: Long wait times, limited availability info, difficulty reordering Users want:reviews, orders details
